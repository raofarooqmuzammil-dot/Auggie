Company Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Our Mission

Auggie Medical Transportation is committed to providing safe, reliable, punctual, and compassionate non-emergency medical transportation (NEMT) to individuals across Downriver Michigan and Metro Detroit. Every passenger is treated with dignity and respect.

2. Driver Standards

All Auggie Medical Transportation drivers are required to:

  • Hold a valid Michigan driver's license
  • Pass a comprehensive background check
  • Be BLS (Basic Life Support) certified
  • Be First Aid certified
  • Complete patient handling and safety training
  • Maintain a professional, courteous demeanor at all times

3. Vehicle Standards

All vehicles in our fleet are:

  • Regularly inspected and maintained
  • Cleaned and sanitized between rides
  • Equipped with wheelchair ramps or stretcher capability where applicable
  • ADA compliant for accessible transport
  • Fully insured and licensed

4. Passenger Safety

The safety of every passenger is our top priority. Our drivers are trained to assist with boarding and alighting, secure wheelchairs properly, and respond to medical situations calmly while contacting emergency services if required.

⚠️ Our service is non-emergency transport only. In a medical emergency, please call 911 immediately.

5. HIPAA Compliance

Auggie Medical Transportation respects the privacy and confidentiality of all passengers. We comply with HIPAA regulations and do not share personal health information with any third parties without written consent.

6. Conduct Policy

We reserve the right to refuse or terminate service if a passenger or accompanying person:

  • Is verbally or physically abusive toward the driver
  • Is under the influence of alcohol or illegal substances
  • Engages in behavior that poses a safety risk

In such cases, no refund will be issued.

7. Service Area

We primarily serve Wayne County, including Taylor, Dearborn, Detroit, and surrounding Downriver communities. Out-of-area trips may be available upon request and may incur additional charges.
