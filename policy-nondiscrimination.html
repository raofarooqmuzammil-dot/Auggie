Non-Discrimination Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Our Commitment

Auggie Medical Transportation provides equal, respectful, and dignified service to all passengers regardless of race, color, religion, national origin, sex, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

1. Equal Access to Services

We do not discriminate in the provision of transportation services on the basis of:

  • Race, color, or national origin
  • Religion or creed
  • Sex or gender identity
  • Age
  • Physical or mental disability
  • Sexual orientation
  • Veteran or military status
  • Immigration or citizenship status

2. Accessibility

Our fleet is equipped to serve passengers with mobility challenges, including wheelchair users and those requiring stretcher transport. We are committed to providing accessible transportation to all individuals who need it.

3. ADA Compliance

Auggie Medical Transportation complies with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA). We provide reasonable accommodations to ensure all passengers can access our services safely and comfortably.

4. Language Access

We make every effort to communicate effectively with passengers who have limited English proficiency. Please contact us in advance if you have specific communication needs.

5. How to Report a Concern

If you believe you have been subjected to discrimination or unequal treatment, please contact us immediately:

  • 📧 Email: auggiemedicaltransportatin@yahoo.com

All complaints will be taken seriously and investigated promptly.
